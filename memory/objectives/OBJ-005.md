## Title

## Description

Operate a second concurrent engineering governance initiative using AEOS under overlapping operational conditions with OBJ-004. This objective validates AEOS under concurrent objective pressure, shared reviewer capacity, cross-objective coordination overhead, and governance scheduling tension. The initiative simulates "Platform Security & Compliance Audit Program" — a parallel engineering coordination effort that must run simultaneously with OBJ-004 while sharing the same governance infrastructure, reviewer pool, and operational cadence.

## Business Context

AEOS has validated single-objective operational pressure (OBJ-004) with 3 concurrent tasks, active incidents, and open escalations. This objective introduces a second ACTIVE objective running concurrently to validate: reviewer contention between objectives, cross-objective coordination overhead, governance scheduling pressure, operational prioritization tension, escalation accumulation across objectives, and shared lifecycle maintenance burden. This is the first validation of AEOS under true concurrency.
