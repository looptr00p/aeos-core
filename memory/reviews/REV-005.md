## Title

Cross-Artifact Continuity Review

## Status

APPROVED WITH OBSERVATIONS

## Type

Continuity Consistency Review

## Severity

MEDIUM

## Description

Review of cross-artifact traceability consistency and coordination overhead under active multi-task lifecycle pressure (TASK-004/005/006, REV-004, INC-001, ESC-002, HND-004).

## Scope

- Validate all traceability references resolve correctly.
- Assess coordination overhead between concurrent active artifacts.
- Identify lifecycle drift risks.
- Evaluate handoff context completeness.

## Findings

### Finding 1: Coordination Overhead Increases Non-Linearly with Active Artifacts

At 3 active tasks, 2 reviews, 1 incident, 1 escalation, and 1 handoff (8 active artifacts total), the number of cross-references grows to approximately 20-25 individual links. Each link must be maintained manually. This is manageable but becomes cognitively expensive at 12+ active artifacts.

**Severity**: MEDIUM
**Recommendation**: Consider traceability assistance template for objectives with 4+ concurrent tasks.

### Finding 2: Governance Repetition Across Tasks Creates Friction

TASK-004, TASK-005, and TASK-006 all contain nearly identical governance requirements sections. This is intentional (each task must be self-contained) but creates governance repetition that contributes to review fatigue.

**Severity**: LOW
**Recommendation**: No structural change. This is a known trade-off between self-containment and conciseness.

### Finding 3: Lifecycle Drift Risk is Real but Manageable

With TASK-004/005/006 all IN_PROGRESS and no closure criteria met, there is a risk that tasks remain open indefinitely without explicit checkpoint reviews. The escalation (ESC-002) partially mitigates this by flagging review overhead, but does not force task closure.

**Severity**: MEDIUM
**Recommendation**: Establish explicit checkpoint reviews for objectives with 3+ IN_PROGRESS tasks after 2 operational cycles.

### Finding 4: Handoff Context is Sufficient but Dense

HND-004 must capture context for 3 tasks, 2 reviews, 1 incident, and 1 escalation. The handoff template supports this but the resulting document becomes dense. Future resumption requires careful reading.

**Severity**: LOW
**Recommendation**: Consider adding a "quick resume" summary section to handoffs for objectives with 3+ active artifacts.

## Decision

APPROVED WITH OBSERVATIONS

Cross-artifact continuity is maintained. Observations document emerging scaling patterns that should be monitored but do not require immediate structural changes.
