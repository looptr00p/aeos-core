## Key Finding: Recovery Capacity Grows Slower Than Pressure Accumulation

The most important finding from this cycle is that recovery capacity grows slower than governance pressure accumulation. This is an inherent property of governance-first systems: accumulation only requires new issues (fast), while recovery requires resolution of existing issues (slow). This asymmetry means that governance debt will always accumulate under sustained pressure unless accumulation rate is reduced or resolution capacity is increased.
